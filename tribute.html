Jiang Zemin playing the flute. He is regarded as a talented leader.

Here is a brief time line of Jiang Zemin:

  • 1926 - born in Yangzhou, Jiangsu, China.
  • 1946 - joined CPC
  • 1985 - became the mayor of Shanghai.
  • 1993 - 2003 - changed China.
  • 2016 - became an Internet celebrity.
